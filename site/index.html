🪨 Earth · flagship

Oklahoma Well Records - Digitization Pipeline

Research project digitizing 570,953 scanned oil & gas well records (1911–2024): OCR, OpenCV grid detection, and U-Net segmentation resolve each well against a 4.5M-cell PLSS PostgreSQL database, with human-in-the-loop corrections applied automatically via GitHub Actions. ~4,900 wells on a live map and growing. Literally bending earth data.
